This is AI

The real magic powering our apps, our secret source. We leverage cutting-edge AI technologies to deliver unparalleled user experiences that are finely tuned to consumer behavior. As Codeway, we committed ourselves to pioneering the field of AI-supported mobile apps.

Face Reenactment

We are currently exploring the literature of 3D mesh-based deep learning methods to better transfer the face expressions and movements that lies naturally in the 3D space. Powering FaceDance

Diffusion Models

We train our own generative diffusion models fine-tuned from open source models. We believe that as GANs, a lot of potential image editing applications can be done in the latent space of pre-trained diffusion models. Powering Wonder and VOI

Large Language Models

We primarily use the Chat-GPT API to power our apps, such as Ask AI and Type AI. Additionally, we train models to tailor them to specific occasions, such as serving as a teaching assistant - Nerd AI - or impersonating beloved celebrities - Charsis AI-
